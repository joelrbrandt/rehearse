What is HelpMeOut?

HelpMeOut is a research project of the Stanford HCI Group. It helps users of Processing to correct bugs in their programs.

How do I use HelpMeOut?

HelpMeOut requires an augmented version of the Processing IDE. You can download builds for Windows and OS X from rehearse.stanford.edu/build.

Where can I go from here?

Take a look at a list of fixes that need explanations, or a list of all bug fixes we have collected.
